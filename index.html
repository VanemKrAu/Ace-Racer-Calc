注意事项：Notes:
  • 关于开局立刻获得能量的被动如何填写：开局获得的充能（如MINI JCW被动）和起步基础充能是两个东西！请勿将开局后获得的能量填入起步基础充能中！请填入“扩展自充能附加模块”中的“绑定到【大招释放】的自充能”中的“首发附加（开局充能等）”输入框中。How to fill in the passive ability that grants energy immediately at the start of a match: The energy gained at the start (e.g., MINI JCW passive) is different from the starting base energy! Do not fill the energy gained at the start into the starting base energy field. Instead, fill it into the 'Initial Bonus (starting energy charge, etc.)' input box under 'Extended Self-Charge Add-on Module' -> 'Self-Charge Bound to [Ultimate Cast]'.
  • 关于偶发无法访问的情况：由于网站部署在境外服务器（GitHub）上，且部分省市运营商会对GitHub做间歇性SNI阻断（具体表现为昨天访问的好好的，今天突然进不去，过了一小会自己又好了），因此有小概率出现无法访问的情况。目前网站接入了第三方中转节点，但依旧存在极小的访问失败概率，这属于正常现象，遇到这种情况时，多刷新几次即可进入网站。另外，网站是无后端运行的，因此在域名过期前基本不可能突然关门（除非被GFW主动阻断），请放心使用。Regarding occasional access issues: Since the site is hosted on overseas servers (GitHub), some regional ISPs may intermittently block SNI - meaning it works fine one day, then suddenly won't load the next, only to fix itself shortly after. So there's a small chance you might not be able to reach it. We've now added third-party relay nodes, but there's still a very slim chance of access failure. That's perfectly normal - just refresh a few times and you'll get in. Also, because the site runs entirely on the frontend with no backend, it's basically not going to disappear before the domain expires (unless the GFW actively blocks it). So rest assured.
  • 更新计划：网站现在和未来都无计划做出选择预设车辆后一键填写数值的功能，这样会让时效性变差且维护周期大大提高，与网站“无后端运行”、“自由定制”、“可长期运行”的初衷相悖。Update Plan: Currently and in the future, the site has no plans to implement a feature that automatically fills in values by selecting a preset vehicle. This would reduce timeliness and greatly increase the maintenance cycle, contradicting the site's original intention of 'no backend operation,' 'customize freely,' and 'long-term operation'.
